Game of Life

In this project, I am working on the Conways Game of Life implementation.

This scrips consists in represent a cells group that have rules for surviving or not in their universe each genaration.

The most comum rules were created by John Conway, the creator of this game. The rules are: each live cell needs to have 2 or 3 lives neighbors to survive the next generation. If a live cell has less than 2 live neighbors, they die by lonelyness. If it has more than 3, it dies by starvation. A death cell can by ressucited by having 3 lives neigbors in the last genaration.

How to Play?

  1. CLick on a cell to give it life. After create your pattern you can press Play button.
  2. The Pause button will pause the actual generation allowing you to change live or death celss to run again click on Play.
  3. The Clean All button clean all screen and reset the Genaration Counter.
  4. The + Gen/s and - Gen/s control the generation per second rate.
  5. The Random button generate a randomic pattern of live cells
